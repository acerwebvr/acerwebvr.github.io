Pretend City is a children's museum found in Irvine, California. Designed to look like a miniature city, Pretend City's 17 interactive exhibits teach children foundational skills in math, reading, and science while fostering their creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills. Pretend City's exhibits include a bank, dentist office, and grocery store.
